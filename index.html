How this works

LinkedIn's default job search sorts by relevance, so fresh postings get buried under ones with 50+ applicants already. This tool builds a search link that sorts by newest first and filters to only recent posts, so you see jobs before the crowd does.

  • Posted within sets how recent a job must be (15 minutes to 24 hours, or a custom window).
  • Workplace narrows to on-site, remote, or hybrid. Leave none selected to include all.
  • Open jumps straight to the filtered LinkedIn search. Copy grabs the link to paste anywhere.
  • Save this search keeps your keywords, location, and filters as a chip for one tap next time.
  • Share chips copies a link containing your saved searches. Send it to someone and when they open it, those searches import into their saved list automatically.
